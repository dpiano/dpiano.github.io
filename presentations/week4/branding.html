Parrot Chocolatiers

"Putting the squawk in squawklet"

Join our Squawk! At Parrot Chocolatiers, we are committed to producing high quality chocolate, containing the richest flavour.We use 100% fair trade cocoa. We are dedicated to spreading awareness about the working and living conditions in developing countries that harvest cocoa.

Competitors

Brands

    Hershey's
    Cadbury
    Nestle
    Camino (fair-trade organic chocolate bars)

Strengths

Hershey's, Cadbury and Nestle all hold huge amounts of the chocolate industry marketshare. They have generated huge profits and have effective ad campaigns. Camino is a less-known brand but they have built their brand on ethical practices that their much larger competitors do not use.

Weaknesses

Hershey's, Cadbury and Nestle all view profit as the utmost importance, but that comes at the expense of their workers who are not treated fairly. Camino needs to focus more money and effort on their ad campaigns and the mediums they choose to get their message to their target audience.

Logo Inspiration

How the logo shows what the company is about

The logo I have made exemplifies the company's fun and loving attitude towards the products they sell. It gives feelings of happiness and well-being through the colour and texture use.

Why the logo is effective for the brand

The logo is effective because it follows the colour set used across all the brand's advertising and online presence. It also reflects the name of the company by using a parrot.

How the branding/logo makes us stand out

The branding and logo make this company stand out from the rest because it features an animal in the logo, unlike the competitors, and in my opinion provides much more visually appealing product packaging and overall brand image. This is a combination of the company's ethical practices and their use of vibrant colours and designs within their branding.
